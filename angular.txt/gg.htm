INVERSION OF CONTROL

  1. The general term of inversion control is "dependency injection".
  2. The word looks very complicated but it is quite easy to use.
  3. Directives,filters,animations concepts uses "dependency  injection technique" for their implementation.
  4. This makes components reusable and testable.
  5. Example:when i want to eat biryani ,i should search for a good restaurant and i should go there.This is a time taking process.Instead of that,i can open zomato or swiggy app to book from home.they will bring the biryani from the desired restaurant.This is called dependeny on service.The service get instantly served.
  6. In angular when you define a controller or a service you will need to specify the dependences of it in this way:
    app.controller("my_controller", function( X, B, C ) {..

    do something with X;
    do something with B;
    do something with C;

    .});
